DETAILED ITINERARY

AMAZONIAN FOREST AND THE ANDES
  • Morning visit to the Leymebamba Museum, on of the most interesting museums in Peru, where you can see the collection of more than 200 Inca-Chachapoyas mummies in a superb state of preservation.
  • Furthermore, this museum has the largest collection of Kipus in Peru. The journey from Leymebamba to Cajamarca, passing by the the Marañon Canyon, is one of Peru's most spectacular drives, offering astonishing scenery.
  • The Marañon River forms one of the world's deepest canyons, and you will descend through different ecosystems from ove 10,500 feet to the town of Balsas at 2,790 feet.
  • After crossing the river you then ascend back through the various habitats to nearly 9,850 feet.
  • Overnight in Cajamarca.
    Meals: Breakfast,Boxed-Lunch.
